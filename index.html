About Me

Hello my name is Nathan and I am an aspiring web developer. I am currently involved in a Bootcamp program through the University of Arizona. My goal is to complete this program with a solid foundation in the area of web development and pursue a career in that field. Ever since I was young I have always had an interest in anything and everything computers. This interest led me to building my own personal computers and dabbling in some light coding ventures where I attempted to learn to code myself; however that venture didn't pan out well and I ultimately gave up.


Outside of web development and computers I love the outdoors and enjoy hiking and camping with my family. I recently moved to Arizona and look forward to venturing out and discovering many of the places this state has to offer.
